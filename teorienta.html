Te Orienta nas Férias! Brazil's Best Academic Summer Program.

Pinemoon, an innovative tech startup focused on providing students with their first paid opportunities, has recently developed cutting-edge software for Te Orienta nas Ferias, a prominent summer program dedicated to preparing students for the Brazilian standardized test. This exciting collaboration aims to enhance the educational experience by integrating technology that streamlines study processes and offers personalized learning pathways. The new software features interactive study tools, progress tracking, and resource libraries, enabling students to effectively prepare for the exam while also exploring potential career avenues. By combining Pinemoon's tech expertise with Te Orienta nas Ferias' established network of motivated learners, this partnership not only elevates academic preparation but also provides students with practical, real-world skills.



Our work. Inovation through AI.

Pinemoon has developed an innovative Discord bot for the Te Orienta nas Ferias program, utilizing AI to assist students with problem-solving, learning, and progress tracking. This interactive tool acts as a virtual tutor, providing personalized support and resources in real time. In addition, Pinemoon created a user-friendly website for the program, serving as a central hub for study materials and community engagement. Together, the Discord bot and website enhance the educational experience, empowering students to excel academically while preparing them for future careers.
